# 🌸 Blossom - Privacy Policy

Protecting your data is a top priority. This policy explains exactly what Blossom sees, saves, and deletes.

### 1. What Data We Collect
To function properly, Blossom stores the following data in a secure, local SQLite database:
* **User IDs & Server IDs:** To track your economy balance, gacha inventory, and server XP.
* **Timestamps:** To calculate cooldowns for commands like `/daily`, `/work`, and `/stream`.
* **Interaction Stats:** The number of times you have used social commands (like `/hug` or `/slap`) and who you interacted with.
* **Server Configurations:** For Server Admins, we store the Channel ID you set for Level-Up messages and Bomb drops.

### 2. What We DO NOT Collect
* **Message Content:** Blossom **does not** read, log, or store the content of your messages. She only checks that a message was sent to award XP, and then immediately forgets it.
* **Personal Information:** We do not collect names, emails, or any identifying information outside of your public Discord User ID.

### 3. How Data is Used
Your data is strictly used to power Blossom's gameplay mechanics (saving your coins, storing your character collection, and tracking your levels). Your data is completely private to the bot and is never shared, sold, or distributed to third parties.

### 4. Data Deletion
You have the right to request a complete wipe of your data at any time. If you want your economy balance, gacha collection, and XP permanently deleted from Blossom's database, please join the official Support Server or contact the developer directly (**@Envvy.VT**).
